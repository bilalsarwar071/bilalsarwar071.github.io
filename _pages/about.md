I'm Muhammad Bilal Sarwar—an **Electrical Engineer** and a budding **Computational Scientist** specializing in Applied Computer Science. I'm currently pursuing my Master's degree from the prestigious National [National University of Sciences and Technology (NUST)](https://www.nust.edu.pk/), Islamabad, with a keen interest in **Formal Methods**, **Machine Learning**, **Deep Learning**, **AI Safety**, and **Autonomous Systems**. My master's thesis is **"Formal Analysis of Lane-Changing Algorithms for Autonomous Vehicles Using Probabilistic Model Checking"**.

My academic journey began at [UET Taxila](https://web.uettaxila.edu.pk/), where I completed my Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering with extended majors in Signal Processing and Communications in 2021. This foundational knowledge has played an instrumental role in shaping my perspective on applied sciences and building a strong understanding of core engineering principles.

In the professional sphere, my experience includes a tenure as a **Technical Support Engineer** at ZKTECO Pakistan, where I honed my problem-solving and technical skills from January 2022 to September 2022. I have also served as a **Research Associate** at IGIS, NUST in the **Geo Visual Analytics Lab**. There, I was part of an exciting HEC-funded project, **"An Autonomous IOT Based Approach Towards Monitoring And Subsequently Identifying Invasive Dengue/Zika Vectors Prevalence and Possible Dengue Outbreak Area."** This project stands as a testament to my commitment to utilizing technological advancements for societal welfare.

In my free time, I dive into the fascinating world of research, striving to contribute to the scientific community and broaden my horizons. My vision is to bridge the gap between computational sciences and practical applications, creating solutions that can make a significant difference.

Feel free to browse my site and explore my projects and publications. Let's embark on this technological journey together.
